The Challenge

Critical operations still run
on spreadsheets and emails

Organizations across industries face the same challenge: coordinating complex, multi-team operational procedures with tools that were never designed for real-time critical operations.

No Real-Time Visibility

Teams update spreadsheets and send status emails. By the time leadership sees the update, the situation has already changed.

Manual Coordination

Phone calls, chat messages, and meetings to coordinate between DBA, network, platform, and application teams. Errors slip through.

Audit Gaps

Regulators want evidence of who did what and when. Reconstructing timelines from email threads after the fact is unreliable and time-consuming.

SLA Breaches

Without automated tracking, teams miss SLA deadlines. Escalations happen too late, and recovery time objectives are routinely exceeded.

for all critical operations
